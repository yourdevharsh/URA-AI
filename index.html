See how Ura transforms your MS Word learning experience.

AI Office Tutor is an intelligent desktop assistant designed to help you work faster and more confidently in Microsoft Word and Excel. It combines real-time screen capture, YOLO-based UI detection, and a local LLM (DeepSeek or Gemma via Ollama) to understand your questions and instantly identify the exact feature you need. When you type a query, the assistant analyzes your request, determines the correct Office tool, and brings Word or Excel to the front. It then captures a live screenshot, detects the relevant toolbar button, and displays a transparent on-screen overlay that highlights it in real time. Alongside this visual guidance, the chatbot provides clear, step-by-step instructions generated by the local AI model. Everything runs fully offline with no cloud processing, giving you a fast, private, and interactive way to learn and navigate Microsoft Office like a pro.
